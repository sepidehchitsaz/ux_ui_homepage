Hi, I'm Sepideh

UX/UI Designer with Experience in Linguistics and Behavioral Therapy

Persistant, Problem Solver, and Passionate to Help People

With extensive experience in behavioral research, the design flaws can be easily identified during the usability testing which is essential in the developing/redesigning of any website and application. Sepideh always gets inspired by the beauty of nature in designing my works.

Featured Works

TaskMaster

TaskMaster was designed to assist individuals with autism who struggle with daily hygiene tasks. Many of these users do enjoy playing on an electronic device. Therefore, by implementing an application, their attention might be gained and being motivated to do these tasks on their own.

Save The Frogs

The goal of this project was to redesign the website based on the usability testing results to bring in more awareness, volunteer interest, and donations for the betterment of amphibian conservation efforts.

Click

Click is a knowledge-sharing app for photographers of all levels. Through Click, photographers will be able to find photography locations for their next shoot as well as suggest and review locations themselves while sharing their photos.
